Our Mission

Rock N Second Chances Rescue is a non-profit organization dedicated to saving dogs from over populated, high kill shelters. We work with committed volunteers, fosters, veterinarians and other rescues partnering to find safe new homes for each dog we save. Partnering with like-minded recues and organizations to achieve a common goal of saving and rehoming recused animals. Providing dogs with temporary care through our foster programs and /or boarding facility. Ensuring the dogs rescued receive proper care using our local veterinarian services. Locating successful placement by using our application and interview process. Provide education to evaluate and train shelter dogs Educating the public and adaptors on responsible pet care and relationships. Helping senior citizens with veterinarian care when a need is made / requested. Finding those special dogs that can become trained as emotional support companions. Long term goal is to expand to include other animals in need of rescue. Rock N Second Chances Rescue seeks to bring a better life to both a dog in need and a family seeking that special friend. No funds that Rock N Second Chances Rescue acquire will be used to benefit a single individual, with the exception in exchange for services benefitting Rock N Second Chances Rescue. If for some reason this non-profit should fail any funds remaining will be donated to another like-minded non-profit rescue.
